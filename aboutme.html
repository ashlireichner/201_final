About Me

An Intro to My Life.

I come from a vibrant and loving family, with two sisters, one brother, and two amazing parents who have always been my pillars of support. My father's roots trace back to Carlsbad, California, a coastal town known for its beautiful beaches and laid-back lifestyle. On the other hand, my mother's family hails from Sonora, Mexico, where they continue to reside, preserving rich traditions and cultural values. Growing up, I was fortunate to experience the warmth and unity that comes from such a diverse familial background, blending the best of both worlds.

My family in Cancun, Meixco.

Growing up in California instilled in me a deep appreciation for the outdoors, especially the exhilarating worlds of surfing and skating. The state's diverse landscapes, from its sun-kissed beaches to its bustling skate parks, became my playgrounds. Surfing taught me the rhythm of the ocean and the thrill of riding waves, while skating offered freedom and creativity on land. These activities not only kept me active but also connected me to a community that shares a passion for adventure and the great outdoors.

Being raised amidst the rich tapestry of Mexican and Californian cultures has profoundly shaped my identity. From celebrating traditional Mexican festivals with my maternal relatives to embracing the dynamic and diverse culture of California, I've learned the importance of heritage and adaptability. This blend of cultures has taught me to value family, community, and the beauty of diverse traditions, all of which continue to influence my worldview and personal values.
